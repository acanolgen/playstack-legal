Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

PlayStack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: PlayStack is designed to be privacy-first. We do not require you to provide personal information such as your name or email address to use the core features of the app. If you use "Sign in with Apple," we receive a unique identifier and, optionally, your email address as provided by Apple.

User-Generated Content (Images): If you choose to customize your profile by uploading an avatar or image, these files are stored securely on our cloud servers (Google Cloud Storage) to ensure they are available across your devices. We do not use these images for any other purpose than displaying them within the app.

Game Tracking Data: Information about your game library, battle passes, and play sessions is stored securely in our database (Firebase) to provide personalization features across your devices.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Firebase: For secure data storage and authentication.
  • RevenueCat: To manage premium subscriptions and verify entitlements safely.
  • Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics: To understand app usage and improve features. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Children's Privacy

PlayStack does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
